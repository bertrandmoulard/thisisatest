What is TeaTree?

TeaTree is a note-taking webapp designed for people managers. It helps you document your conversations with your reports, track their goals and suggest discussion topics

Notes

A simple and intuitive note-taking interface. Take notes during or after your 1:1 and ad hoc conversations. Write down discussion topics in advance. Well documented manager/report relationships will help you stay on top of your management work and can be crucial in many situations.

Suggested Topics

TeaTree will suggest discussion topics that are important to a productive manager/report relationships. This feature will help you add some variety to your conversations and sometimes get out of your (or your reports') comfort zone.

Goal tracking

We believe every employees should have clear, measurable, time bound goals. TeaTree will help you track those goals and check on progress periodically.

Why do I need it?

The path to becoming a better manager

  • Show that you care

    Taking good notes and using those notes is an essential part of being a good manager. The first benefit is to show your reports that you care about them by remembering what was previously discussed and avoiding asking them to repeat themself. Checking on previous notes before a one on one and following up on past discussion topics will show them that you're listening.

  • Be prepared for hard times

    Disputes between managers and their reports are unfortunately a fact of work life. Being prepared in those situation is crucial and solid documentation of conversations and events is fundamental. Your searchable, dated notes will be an invaluable asset while navigating those difficult situations.

  • Commit to a process

    Managers often take lose notes in Google Docs or other note-taking apps. TeaTree will provide a central place and easy to use interface for all management related notes. Having a dedicated tool will not only make it easier to be a better manager, but also encourage you to stick to good practices.

  • Embrace hard conversations

    TeaTree will suggest discussion topics. Not all of them will lead to hard conversations, but all of them aim to nurture productive and complete manager/report relationships. Everyone has things they don't like to talk about, but it doesn't mean they shouldn't be discussed. TeaTree aims to provide gentle nudges to encourage you to be a more thorough manager through a wide range of topics.

  • Help your reports hit their goals

    Work goals are often set and forgotte. TeaTree lets you enter goals for each report, track progress and take notes. This feature makes it very easy for managers to remember to talk about goals with their reports periodically, ask about progress and what they can do to help their reports achive their objectives.
